# M11 — Mixed Emotions

*What feelings become when they do not flow — stored, time-displaced, story-laden compounds of two or three feelings tangled with the original event that froze them.*

![Map of Mixed Emotions — Possibility Management map](../Maps/M11.png)

**What it is.** A mixed emotion is a stored state that is part feeling, part memory, part story — two or three of the four feelings tangled with the past event that froze them. Not a pure feeling; not just a stronger one. The default adult condition is mixed-emotion soup. Pure feelings last minutes; mixed emotions last years. The way out is *unmixing* — naming the components one at a time and letting each move as itself through the Emotional Healing Process (M12).

**At a glance.** Sadness + fear → collapse, hopelessness · Anger + sadness → resentment, bitterness · Fear + anger → anxiety, defensive rage · Joy + sadness → nostalgia, bittersweet · Sadness + suppressed anger + fear of own anger → depression · Fear + anger + sadness, all numbed → "stress". Storage states, not feelings — they loop, linger, drain energy. "Frustration," "overwhelm," "burnout" are stories about mixed emotions, not feelings. The sharpest test: longer than five minutes means stored, not present-time.
