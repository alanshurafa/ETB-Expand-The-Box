# M20 — Map of Possibility

*The meta-map that locates every other PM distinction — possibility named not as a list of options but as a space the learner enters on purpose, by holding context, becoming liquid, and asking what cannot be unasked.*

![Map of Possibility — Possibility Management map](../Maps/M20.png)

**What it is.** Most people live inside the causal paradigm — the territory of *what is* — where *what could be* shows up only as fantasy, a way of not being where you are without ever leaving. The Map of Possibility names a separate country: possibility is not a list of options the world hands you, it is a space you enter on purpose, empty until someone steps in. Once inside, what was invisible becomes available — not because the world changed, but because the Box stopped filtering it. This is the meta-map: it locates every other PM distinction as the room they all stand in.

**At a glance.** Possibility is a SPACE, not a list → context, not content. Three keys to enter → hold a context · be in liquid state · ask a dangerous question (simultaneous, not sequential). Possibility vs fantasy → fantasy floats and never acts; possibility lands and originates action. Empty until someone steps in → populated by declarations, dangerous questions, bright-principle sourcing. Distinct from manifestation → the space delivers nothing; it makes new action available, which then runs on ordinary cause and effect. A practitioner enters on purpose.
