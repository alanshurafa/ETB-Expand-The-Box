# M12 — Emotional Healing Process

*The structured PM practice for completing a stored emotion so its energy returns — five positions, non-linear, no script, requires a witness for anything beyond small material, NOT trauma processing.*

![Map of Emotional Healing Process — Possibility Management map](../Maps/M12.png)

**What it is.** The Emotional Healing Process (EHP) is the structured PM practice for completing a stored emotion — a moment-to-moment, non-linear discovery journey with no script, navigated with a witness. It is for ordinary stored emotional residue (the teacher who shamed you, the parent who could not receive your anger); the unfinished feeling completes, the old decision unmixes, and bound energy returns as relief and lightness. **The EHP is NOT trauma processing — trauma (flashbacks, dissociation, abuse history) needs a qualified clinician with a trauma-built protocol (EMDR, somatic experiencing, IFS), because treating trauma with the EHP can re-traumatize.** It requires a witness for anything beyond small material.

**At a glance.** Position 0 → foundation (center, ground, bubble, cube) · 1 → make contact with the witness · 2 → navigate to liquid state, sound before words · 3 → work the original event: block, old/new decision, unmix · 4 → start closing, return to room · 5 → the end, container dissolves, energy available. Witness's role → present, grounded, no fixing, no advising, no sending love; being seen IS the medicine. Expressing (acts out at a person, damage) vs processing (completes in a held container, closure).
