# M39 — Map of Optional Healing Process

*The EHP path drawn as six stations, with the load-bearing word in its own title — optional. Nobody heals you, nothing makes you heal, and the choice is the first position before the first position.*

![Map of Optional Healing Process — Possibility Management map](../Maps/M39.png)

**What it is.** Six stations down the page, drawn as a path: the golden cube of workspace at the top, two figures making contact, sounds before words, the liquid-state cloud, the old-decision/new-decision arrow, and a heart at the close. (The map numbers its stations 1 through 6; the course names the same stations Position 0 through Position 5.) The word to sit with is in the title: optional. Nobody heals you. Nothing makes you heal. A stored emotion can sit in an emotional body for sixty years while the person stays functional, and plenty do exactly that. An EHP begins with a person choosing, out loud, to complete something, and "nothing today" is a valid answer. Every later position stays inside that consent. This is not a soft disclaimer; it is what separates healing work from the things that get done *to* people.

**At a glance.** Six stations as a path → cube, contact, sounds-before-words, liquid state, old/new decision, the heart at the close · The title word → optional: nobody heals you, nothing makes you heal · The choice is the first position before the first position → "What can I do for you?" is a question, and "nothing today" is a valid answer · Optional ≠ free → the cost of skipping is the storage itself, energy staying bound and the loop still running · No one can be processed against their will → the loving move toward an unconsenting person is to keep being someone they could one day choose · **Study-level only here** → the doing lives behind the gate.

## How to explain it verbally

This map draws the healing process as six stations down a path: the golden cube at the top, two people making contact, sounds before words, the liquid-state cloud, the old-decision-to-new-decision arrow, and a heart at the close. But the word to actually sit with is right there in the title: optional. Nobody heals you. Nothing makes you heal. A stored emotion can sit in a body for sixty years while the person stays perfectly functional, and many do. An EHP only begins when a person chooses it out loud, and "nothing today" is a completely valid answer; every position after that stays inside that consent. This is not a soft disclaimer. It is the line that separates healing work from the things that get done *to* people. The choice is the first position before the first position.

**If you only remember one thing:** healing is optional by design and begins only with the person's own out-loud choice; consent is the first position before the first position.
