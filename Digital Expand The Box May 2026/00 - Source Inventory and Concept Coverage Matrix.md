# 00 — Source Inventory and Concept Coverage Matrix

**Purpose.** This document maps every Possibility Management concept the course teaches to the source material it draws on. It is the audit trail for the course design and the first place a future facilitator should look when checking whether a distinction was reconstructed faithfully.

**Source treatment.** All source folders are read-only. The course was written by *reading* the source material as inspiration — never by copying, moving, editing, or unzipping it.

## E. Provenance Note

Every map photographed by hand in the source folder belongs to the **lineage of Clinton Callahan and the Possibility Management community**. The course rewrites those distinctions in clean facilitator language — it does not reproduce the photographs or transcribe the videos. Where a Map Note refers a learner to a visual, it points back to the read-only source path. If this course is ever distributed externally, the maps and lineage attribution must be carried with it.
