# M43 — Letting Your Heart Speak

*The gate between Adult speech and Possibility Speaking — speech routed from the heart instead of the mind's editor, the channel that has to open before the fourth speaking becomes reachable.*

![Letting Your Heart Speak — Possibility Management map](../Maps/M43.png)

**What it is.** Between Adult speech and Possibility Speaking sits a gate most adults keep shut: speech that comes from the heart instead of the mind's editor. The mind drafts, censors, polishes, and delivers something defensible. The heart, given the channel, says the unpolished true sentence: shorter, warmer, sometimes shaky. The map draws the two routes. Heart speech is not a performance of vulnerability and not emotional venting; it is what becomes available when you stop running every sentence through the editor first. Possibility Speaking (the fourth of the 4 Speakings, M15) is almost never reachable through the editor, so if you want the fourth speaking, this gate opens first.

**At a glance.** Two routes → through the mind's editor (drafted, censored, defensible) vs through the heart (unpolished, true, sometimes shaky) · Sits between Adult speech and Possibility Speaking → the gate the fourth speaking needs · Not a performance of vulnerability, not venting → what is left when the editor stops pre-screening · Possibility Speaking is almost never reachable through the editor → open this gate first · Heart speech is shorter and warmer than the defensible version the editor would have produced.

## How to explain it verbally

Between ordinary clear Adult speech and the fourth speaking, Possibility Speaking, there is a gate that most adults keep shut. It is the difference between speech that comes from the mind's editor and speech that comes from the heart. The editor drafts, censors, polishes, and hands you something defensible. The heart, if you give it the channel, says the shorter, warmer, sometimes shaky true sentence instead. And here is why it matters: Possibility Speaking is almost never reachable through the editor. So if you want that fourth speaking, this is the gate that has to open first. Just be clear what heart speech is not. It is not a performance of being vulnerable and it is not emotional venting. It is simply what is left when you stop running every sentence past the editor before you say it.

**If you only remember one thing:** Possibility Speaking is unreachable through the mind's editor; letting the heart have the channel is the gate that opens first.
